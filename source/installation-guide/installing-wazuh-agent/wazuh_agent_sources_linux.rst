| CentOS                        | :doc:`6 or greater <wazuh_agent_sources_linux_rpm>`                       |
+-------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Debian                        | :doc:`7 or greater <wazuh_agent_sources_linux_deb>`                       |
+-------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Fedora                        | :doc:`22 or greater <wazuh_agent_sources_linux_fedora>`                   |
+-------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------+

+-------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------+
